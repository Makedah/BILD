    Corporate Solutions

    ATTRACT, ENGAGE & TRANSACT WITH SUBCONTRACTORS AND DIVERSE SUPPLIERS BY SHOWCASING YOUR PROJECT OPPORTUNITIES IN THIS DYNAMIC & COLLABORATIVE ONLINE COMMUNITY.

    Empowering corporations to do More business within their local communities.  BILD helps  streamline the identification, communication and on-boarding of local small and diverse vendors and suppliers within the construction industry.  The portal provides accurate and up-to-date vendor information allowing corporations to view and target specific companies in their local area based on their selection criteria.

    CENTRALIZED SUPPLIER INFORMATION

    Search and view robust profiles of small, women, minority and disabled-veteran owned companies in the various communities you do business including:

    • Project History – Vendor profiles include an up-to-date portfolio of past and present project work
    • Company Details – Ownership, size, service areas, business classifications (NAICS & SIC codes)
    • Financial & Insurance Data
    • Certifications

    CONNECT AND TRANSACT

    • Secure and Track Communication with Vendors
    • Email, Chat, Virtual Meetings and/or Video Stream Info with Vendors
    • Promote Available Opportunities and Receive Bids Electronically
    • Streamline On-Boarding Process and Approvals

    REPORTS & ANALYTICS

    • Monitor Contract Compliance &  Track Inclusion Goals
    • Powerful and Informed Decision Making through Predictive & Data Analytics
    • System Integration
