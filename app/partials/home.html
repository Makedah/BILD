Hot New Bid Opportunities


Five-Story Residential Development, plus Neighborhood Retail and Above-Ground Parking

Developer is seeking firms interested in providing professional services, including engineering and related services, for the design and construction of the Project. The Project is comprised of 135 residential units, 10,000 square feet of neighborhood retail, a 41 space above-ground parking structure, and associated amenity spaces. The design features a 5 story Type III building over podium, Type V townhomes and a publicly accessible pedestrian pathway on the Western side of the site. The building will include 45 one-bedroom units, 45 two-bedroom units, 45 three-bedroom units, and all will be affordable to households earning 50% AMI or less, pursuant to the City and County of San Francisco’s Mayor’s Office of Housing & Community Development (“MOHCD”) guidelines.

The RFQ, RFP and related documents can be found at: http://bit.ly/MissionBa

RFQ and RFP Submittals Due: Monday, December 15, 2014 by 4:00PM PST

Small Business Enterprises (SBEs), LBEs, MBEs, and WBEs are encouraged to respond
