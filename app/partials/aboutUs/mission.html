BILD's Mission - We empower private, public corporations and government entities to discover, connect and transact with small & diverse businesses.

Why We Exist

The Founders of BILD started their individual journeys years ago when both worked in the fields of construction, procurement and diversity. Upon introduction from a mutual friend who knew the two would make a great team, Makedah and Vivian joined forces. They both experienced first-hand the numerous pain points encountered by large corporations, government entities and small businesses seeking to work together.

Some of the common challenges that persist to this day are:

  • Lack of Centralized System
  • Antiquated Methodologies
  • Disparate Systems
  • Lack of/Outdated Data about Small Businesses
  • Lack of Use of Technology

Our Solution

Our is to make your life easier by eliminating time, money, duplication, effort and frustration spent searching for each other and provide a smoother on-boarding process. Business Intelligence Logistics Development Corporation (BILD) is a collaborative cloud-based system where we centralize, facilitate and streamline the identification, communication and contracting processes between corporations, government entities & small business.
