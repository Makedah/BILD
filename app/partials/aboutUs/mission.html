BILD’s mission is to “bridge” the gap in the identification, communication and contracting between small business, private corporations and government entities.

Why We Exist

We, the founders of BILD started our journey together working to address the numerous pain points encountered by large corporations, government entities and small businesses seeking to work together.

Some of the common challenges we found across the board to be:

  • Lack of Centralized System
  • Antiquated Methodologies
  • Disparate Systems
  • Lack of/Outdated Data about Small Businesses
  • Lack of Use of Technology

Our Solution

Business Intelligence Logistics Development Corp. (B.I.L.D) is a collaborative cloud-based system where we centralize small business data, facilitate and streamline the identification, communication, and contracting processes between corporations or government entities and small business.
