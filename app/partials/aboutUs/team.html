Makedah Shartukar, Co-Founder & CEO

Makedah Shartukar, is the CEO and owner of the FCOM Group, Inc. She has over 15 years of experience in HR Management and Business Operations from the High-Tech and the Real Estate Default Industry. She has worked with and for a number of Fortune 500 companies like Applied Materials, GMAC, and Bank of America. Makedah has also worked as a consultant with small businesses for the last five years. The culmination of her experiences in Human Resources, Marketing, Sales, and Technology resulted in the vision and creation of Full Circle.

Vivian R Stone, Co-Founder & President

Vivian brings 38 years of Hotel Management, Law Firm Management and Government experience. She served 6 years as a Legal Administrator to mid-size law firms, 9 years as an Executive of Staff & Building Services for the California State Bar receiving recognition from its Board and staff for developing and implementing innovative programs in Diversity Staffing and Procurement, Safety Programs, Training, and Change Management. She then spent 17 years managing her own consulting firm assisting small businesses and legal start-ups secure business and governmental opportunities while creating their company administrative infrastructure before joining Full Circle as a Partner. Her 7 years experience working in the hospitality industry serving as a Hotel Manager for the Hilton Hotel and Le Meridian Hotel Corporation’s instilled in her an exceptional customer service focus and afforded her the opportunity to meet and develop key relationships with some of the world’s most notable people that have proven beneficial to the growth of our organization and to some of our clients.
