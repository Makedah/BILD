Our Team

Makedah Shartukar, Co-Founder & CEO

Makedah’s inclusive and collaborative leadership style, her love of and curiosity of people coupled with the global perspective gained through her African and Filipino heritage and experiences living overseas created the entrepreneurial mind and sharing spirit within her today. She possesses an almost uncanny ability to recognize and connect disparate parts whether it be information, people, ideas or solutions in a manner that has others labeling her as a “Creative Visionary”.

She received her B.S. degree in Business Administration from Cal State University, Hayward before amassing 15 years of experience in the high-tech industry in HR Management and Supplier Diversity with several Silicon Valley corporations. Makedah moved on from Silicon Valley to become a top producing real estate agent that specialized in the Default Industry where she worked with Bank of America, GMAC and Wells Fargo.

Makedah’s entrepreneurial desire could not be denied causing her to throw caution to the winds and leave corporate America to start her own consulting firm focused on supplier diversity and small business inclusion. Her firm worked with large corporations and on major commercial construction projects including the Transbay Transit Center Project, the upcoming historical Ghana National Museum on Slavery and with exciting new start-ups such as Black Girls Code.

Given her first hand experiences running a small business dedicated to connecting small business to large business, her role in corporate America sourcing talent, contractors, suppliers and small business inclusion brought home to her the real time problems connecting small business and large corporations. She had her “AHA!” moment and launched BILD-Connect.com.

Vivian R Stone, Co-Founder & President

Vivian's global perspective comes from growing up in Germany and traveling the world.

Her warm personality, strong leadership style, adventurous spirit, curiosity, and love of people steered her to a career in which she worked her way from the mail room to managing a 1800 room Hilton Hotel in less than 5 years and being the youngest manager of a team opening a 700 room Le Meridien Hotel property, taking it from construction through opening. She moved on to the legal arena managing small and medium law firms, spending 8 years as an executive at the State Bar of California in charge of Construction & Real Property, Procurement & Diversity and Staff Administration & Operations. Having worked for large corporations and State Government left her entrepreneurial thirst unquenched so she utilized those experiences to open and run her own successful small business consulting firm for 17 years. Her corporate and government experience, leadership and indomitable spirit helped her thrive in male dominated positions and garnered her inclusion in "Who's Who in Business" and "Who's Who in Women". Her passion for helping others is why she started her own firm helping small business, it earned her the "Hero" Award from the Mayor of San Francisco, KGO Radio, San Francisco Giants and UCSF Medical Center for saving the life of a renowned scientist and for volunteering her time as an instructor teaching others C.P.R. and First Aid.
